about me!

ah, so you want to learn more...

if so, i suppose that you've come to the right place! i'm admittedly not very great at talking about myself, but i suppose that that will probably change once i have to do it literally all the time here!

that said, hi there! i'm Huldra! i'm aaalmost 21 (in just a few weeks!) and i'm a college student studying Psychology and Sociology. right now things are pretty ambiguous on the pronoun front, but i usually use she/her. i'm not too picky though seeing as my gender is naught but a facet of the mysterious and complex cosmic entity that i am. or, at least, that's how i like to think of it, hehehe. i don't really like they/them unless it's being used in the collective sense, though.

i belong to a wide assortment of various marginalized communities! obviously, i'm trans, as implied both by the weird gender stuff and by the fact that i own a website, but i'm also a disabled plural queer lesbian therian kinnie. i'm also probably some other stuff that would make your grandpa rather cross with me that i don't even know about!

a brief aside before continuing, by the way...

i accept a lot of people, but i have to draw the line when it comes to things that i simply find wrong. hopefully you don't fall under any of these categories, but i ask you to please leave my website if you fall under the following criteria:

  • MAP / NOMAP / etc.
  • proship / comship / etc.
  • TERF / GC / etc.
  • anti-neopronouns
  • staunch sysmeds and transmeds

hopefully you've made it out alive :P

also, if you dislike any of the things i identify with... okay, good for you, but chances are this site just isn't for you.


typology and personality test results

enneagram results

i took two tests on the Eclectic Energies website and got slightly differing results.

a long time ago (junior or senior year in high school i want to say), i took the test and got 4w3 or "The Individualist" with a wing towards "The Achiever".

according to this most recent test, though, i am more of a 6w7 or "The Loyalist" with a wing towards "The Enthusiast".

to make matters even more confusing, i followed the basic enneagram test with the instinctual variant test and it gave me 4w3 again, but this time specifying that my instinctual variant was the sexual variant. i'm not sure about the whole 4w3 thing, but the sexual variant part definitely sounds right, hehe.

interestingly, the type 6 page says that it's somewhat common for type 6s to mistype as type 4s, "especially if they have artistic inclinations", and if i'm not an artist, i have no idea what i am! maybe that's why i keep getting type 4 assignments.

additionally, i scored high in type 9, "The Peacemaker", but i'm not sure if that's as similar to me as type 6 is.


OSPP temperament

according to the OSPP temperaments test, my temperament is melancholic! that means that i'm more of the introverted and analytical type who tends towards depression and introspection.

my second closest temperament is sanguine which i think has an interesting contrast by being... almost the opposite of melancholic? it sort of combines being introverted and withdrawn with being outgoing impulsivity. which, like, i guess is pretty accurate!

the blend of the two, or so i have learned from another website that claims to understand these things, is called "The Diplomat". a melancholic-sanguine person has the need to do things right, figure out what's right, and, at the same time, be accepted by the people around them. very interesting stuff!


MBTI / 16 personalities

i took the classic 16 personalities online test and, according to them, i am an INFP-T, or the Mediator.

this is the same type as many interesting people like Woolf, Kierkegaard, Camus, Tolkien, and Kafka! a lot of musicians i love are INFPs as well! the IDRlabs website (which is also where i found that list of famous INFPs) pithily describes INFPs as "idiosyncratic dreamers with strong imaginations"... which, again, i suppose is right!

the way that 16 personalities themselves describe it, though, is simply as "poetic, kind, and altruistic", which feels a little less exciting and descriptive of myself, especially with the turbulent T at the end of my type.

interestingly, i had an almost even split on the N/S division, scoring a 52% intuitive. if i were an ISFP—well, first of all, i'd be a little upset, since i feel like ISFP looks a lot worse than INFP! either way, it would mean that i'm the "Adventurer", a very artistic type of person who tends to stay in their own lane. honestly, i see how there's a pretty close divide between N/S, since i'm not sure which one i relate to closer!


Pokémon Mystery Dungeon quiz

although i've taken a lot of quizzes already and adding them to the page after the fact, i remembered the quizzes from Pokémon Mystery Dungeon to determine what pokémon you were and i simply had to see if there was a bigger, updated, more "adult" one. sure enough, there was!

you can take this quiz over at this fellow github website here! there's a short and a long version of the quiz so i obviously took the long one, hehe.

the pokémon i got were mudkip and torchic! these are two of my favourite starters so i'm definitely glad to have gotten them.

more specifically, the quiz determined me to be of the rash nature. it said that i'm prone to do things hastily like leave home without locking the door while forgetting my belongings. unfortunately, this is very true! i'm so forgetful when leaving that my roommate from my last year at college would more often expect me to immediately return after leaving than she expected i actually stay out!

in the end, though, i can't complain—that just meant that when she was the one to forget something, i could laugh right back at her! that sounds a little rude... sorry if you're reading this, Luka!

the quiz also seemed to indicate that i was close to the hasty and timid natures. wait, is that why it said i do things hastily...? uh-oh, i better move on before i get any more wrong about this wonderful quiz! you should take it yourself and hopefully you'll understand your results better than i did mine, hehe.

the character who i had an 87% match with was Klaus Hargreeves from The Umbrella Academy who, amazingly, is on my extended kinlist! i'm a reaaally big Klaus enjoyer and feel similar to him in a lot of ways, so it makes sense.

big five test

i'm not as familiar with the big five outside of it simply existing, but on the test, i got a 100% in both openness and neuroticism. basically, i am the very creative type but also the extremely anxious type. hey, i feel like i've seen this kind of result before...

i'll admit that these results may be not be entirely accurate due to the test that i used. don't get me wrong, i trust the website perfectly fine! it's another quiz from IDRlabs, so i think that it's probably valid enough.

the only worry i have with it is that i took the "short" big five test. maybe i'll take a longer one and come back again, though i doubt my results will be very different. in all likelihood, they would just skew slightly more to neurotic and less to openness!
